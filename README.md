#  Impact of Generative AI on Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills Among Undergraduate Students

##  Project Overview

This research project investigates the impact of Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) on the critical thinking and problem-solving skills of undergraduate students. Using survey-based primary data and statistical analysis, the study explores how AI tools influence students' learning habits, decision-making, and academic performance.

##  Objectives

- Examine the relationship between Generative AI usage and critical thinking skills.
- Analyze the impact of AI on students' problem-solving abilities.
- Understand patterns of AI adoption among undergraduate students.
- Generate evidence-based insights using statistical analysis.
